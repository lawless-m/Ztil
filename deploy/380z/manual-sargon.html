Sargon Chess

Starting

A>SARGON

Answer the setup questions:
- Error bell: Y or N
- Difficulty level: 0 (instant) to 6 (slow)
- Play white or black

Making Moves

Enter moves as: E2E4 (from-square to-square)
Columns: A-H (left to right)
Rows: 1-8 (bottom to top, white side)

Tips

- Level 0 is instant, good for testing
- Level 3+ can take a while to think
- Ctrl-C exits to CP/M
